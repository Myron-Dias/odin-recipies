Crispy Chicken Wings

Description

Crispy chicken wings are a popular appetizer and game day snack. They are made by deep-frying or baking chicken wings until the skin is crispy and the meat is cooked through. The wings are often tossed in a sauce or seasoning of some kind before serving.

There are many variations of the recipe, some common methods include: marinating the wings in a mixture of spices and sauces for added flavor, then deep-fry them or bake them in the oven. The wings can also be coated in a mixture of flour, cornstarch, and spices to create a crispy texture. Some popular sauces include buffalo, BBQ, honey mustard, and ranch. They are often served with a side of blue cheese or ranch dressing for dipping. Crispy chicken wings are a great option for parties, game day gatherings, or as a casual meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds chicken wings
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a shallow baking dish with aluminum foil and grease lightly.
  2. Cut tips from chicken wings and discard. Cut between the joint to separate each wing into 2 pieces.
  3. Combine butter and garlic in a small saucepan over low heat; heat until butter is melted and garlic is fragrant, 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Mix Parmesan cheese, parsley, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper together in a shallow bowl.
  5. Dip chicken wing pieces in melted butter mixture; coat with Parmesan cheese mixture in the bowl. Arrange in the prepared baking dish. Drizzle any remaining butter on top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until skin is browned and juices run clear, about 1 hour.
